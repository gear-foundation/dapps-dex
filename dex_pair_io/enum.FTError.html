Enum dex_pair_io::FTError

pub enum FTError {
    InsufficientAmount,
    InsufficientAllowance,
    ZeroRecipientAddress,
    ZeroSenderAddress,
}
Expand description

Fungible token error variants.

Variants§

§

InsufficientAmount

Token owner doesn’t have a sufficient amount of tokens. Or there was the Amount overflow during token minting.

§

InsufficientAllowance

[msg::source()] or operator doesn’t have a sufficient allowance of tokens. Or there was the Amount overflow during allowance increasing.

§

ZeroRecipientAddress

A recipient/operator address is [ActorId::zero()].

§

ZeroSenderAddress

A sender address is [ActorId::zero()].
